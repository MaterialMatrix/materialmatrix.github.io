Services

Design, engineer, and print what needs to work

MaterialMatrix helps clients move from concept to physical reality, whether the starting point is a sketch, a broken part, a list of requirements, or an existing 3D model.

Core offerings

How MaterialMatrix helps clients

We work with inventors, repair teams, student groups, makers, and small businesses that need something useful built, tested, or reproduced with real-world performance in mind.

Concept-to-CAD design

Translate ideas, sketches, and requirements into engineered 3D models that are practical to print and practical to use.

Functional prototyping

Build and refine prototypes for fit, strength, hardware integration, and manufacturability before committing to final output.

Replacement and repair components

Recreate worn or missing parts for non-obsolete machinery, equipment, and tools, including flexible components like seals.

Small-batch production

Produce low-volume runs for client work and in-house product ideas once the design and performance are dialed in.

Typical workflow

From first idea to finished part

The process stays approachable for clients, while the technical thinking stays focused on function, durability, and repeatability.

01

Start with the idea

Review the sketch, broken part, requirement list, or model along with the job it needs to do.

02

Engineer for use

Choose geometry, tolerances, materials, and print strategy based on strength, flexibility, wear, and environment.

03

Prototype and refine

Test fit and function, revise when needed, and validate the part before final production.

04

Deliver the final product

Produce one-off parts or small-batch runs with repeatable settings and practical finishing.
