How to submit a project request

Project requests are handled through Facebook Messenger, but this page now keeps the instructions on-site so visitors know exactly what to send before they leave.

1. Open the MaterialMatrix Facebook page and click Message.

2. Send your idea, photos, sketches, dimensions, files, or part references.

3. Mention what the part needs to do, any material preferences, and your timing if it matters.
