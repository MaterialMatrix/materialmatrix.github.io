Project Requests

All custom project requests are handled through Facebook Messenger. This page is here to help you prepare your request so we can respond faster and more accurately.

How to submit your idea

1. Open the MaterialMatrix Facebook page and click Message.

2. Send your project details, including:

Photos, sketches, or reference images

Dimensions or size requirements

Files (if you have them)

Existing part references (if applicable)

3. Include a short description of:

What the part or design needs to do

Any material preferences (if you have them)

Your timeline or deadline (if it matters)
